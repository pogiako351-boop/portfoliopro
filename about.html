Why We Built This

Savings Calculator UK was built to make financial calculations simple and clear. Many people struggle with understanding how compound interest works and how to compare savings rates. We wanted to create a free tool that shows you the math in plain English.

Our goal is education first. We explain terms like AER, APR, and compound interest so you can make better decisions with your money.

How It Works

All calculations run directly in your browser. We don't store your numbers or collect personal data. The tool uses the standard compound interest formula to estimate future savings based on your inputs.

Remember: this is an estimate only. Actual returns depend on the bank, fees, tax, and whether interest is compounded monthly, quarterly, or annually.

Stay Independent

We don't work for any bank. Our content is written to be unbiased and educational. Some links may be affiliate links, which helps us keep the site free. This never affects the rates or information we show.
